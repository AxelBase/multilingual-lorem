Share Your Settings with a Single Link

Share Your Settings with a Single Link

Published: April 15, 2025

You’ve just created the perfect German placeholder text: 8 paragraphs, HTML lists, with real opening sentences. Now you need to show your client.

Most tools force you to take a screenshot. Not this one.

Every setting — language, quantity, format, variation, even the “start with real sentence” option — is encoded directly into the URL. Click “Share URL” and copy a link that recreates your exact setup for anyone, anywhere.

How It Works

All settings are stored in clean, readable query parameters:

?lang=de&qty=8&unit=p&fmt=html-ul&real=1

Paste that link in Slack, email, Notion, or Figma comments — and the recipient sees exactly what you see. No explanation needed.

Use Cases

  • Design handoff with developers
  • Client presentation decks
  • Team style guide documentation
  • Teaching web accessibility or multilingual design
  • Sharing mockup reviews (“Use this Arabic version”)

No more “Wait, which version was that?”

Privacy Bonus

Since everything happens client-side, your settings are never sent to a server. The URL sharing is safe, private, and instant.

FAQ

Can I bookmark my favorite configs?

Yes! Bookmark the shared link — it will always regenerate the same text.

Do links work offline?

Yes — the app is fully static. Save the page and links still work.

One link. Perfect sync. Better collaboration.
