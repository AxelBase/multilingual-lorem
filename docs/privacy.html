Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

Welcome to the Multilingual Lorem Placeholder Text Generator — a completely client-side, open-source web tool that generates authentic placeholder text in over 15 languages.

We Collect Absolutely Nothing

This tool runs 100% in your browser. When you generate Korean, Arabic, or Portuguese text, nothing is sent to any server. No IP addresses, no browser fingerprints, no usage logs, no cookies, no localStorage persistence — zero data leaves your device.

  • No personal data is ever collected or stored
  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Plausible, Umami, etc.)
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No server-side processing of any kind
  • No cloud storage or backend

How It Actually Works

All language data, word lists, punctuation rules, and generation logic are bundled directly into the app. When you change settings or click “Copy”, everything happens locally using JavaScript. Even URL sharing uses only query parameters — no data is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

There are none. We do not load external fonts, CDNs with tracking, or analytics scripts. The app is fully static and can be used offline after the first load.

Your Data Security

Because no data is transmitted or stored by us, your generated text — even if sensitive — remains completely private. You control your browser; we have no access.

Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe for all ages. No personal information is ever requested or collected.

Compliance

By collecting nothing, we are automatically compliant with GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, and all global privacy laws — because there is no data to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (unlikely), we will update this page with the new effective date.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository or email the maintainer.

We believe tools should respect users from day one. That’s why we built this one with privacy by design — not as an afterthought.
